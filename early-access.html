Delivery Reimagined

Meet the Doselet.

A centimeter-sized, plant-based nanofiber patch applied to the inside of the cheek. It dissolves in seconds and delivers 10mg of NAD+ directly through the buccal mucosa. No needles, no pills, no fuss.

Patients place it, forget it and get on with their day. It’s the daily NAD+ format they’ll actually stick with between visits. We’re thrilled to be the first to bring this delivery format to the nutraceuticals space.

Doselets are sold in 30-packs, making them easy to retail per unit, bundle into a subscription or align with monthly clinic visits.
